What we do

Engine diagnostics & repairs

We use modern diagnostic equipment to scan the vehicle’s onboard computer for error codes, monitor performance data, and detect issues that may not be visible during a basic inspection.

Brake & clutch systems

We inspect, diagnose, and maintain key components including brake pads, discs, calipers, brake fluid, clutch plates, pressure plates, and hydraulic systems. While addressing issues such as reduced braking efficiency, squeaking or grinding noises, soft brake pedals, clutch slipping, difficulty shifting gears, and fluid leaks.

Suspension & steering

We inspect, diagnose, and maintain key suspension and steering components including shock absorbers, struts, springs, control arms, tie rods, and power steering systems while addressing issues such as uneven tyre wear, poor handling, excessive bouncing, steering vibration, and difficulty in steering control.

Transmission & drivetrain

We address common issues like gear slipping, delayed shifting, unusual vibrations, fluid leaks, and noise during acceleration. Using precise diagnostic techniques, we identify the root cause of the problem and provide effective repairs or maintenance.

Electrical systems

We inspect, diagnose, and maintain key electrical components including batteries, alternators, starters, wiring, fuses, and lighting systems while addressing issues such as battery drain, faulty wiring, dim or flickering lights, starting problems, and malfunctioning electrical accessories.

Cooling System Repairs

We inspect, diagnose, and maintain key cooling system components including radiators, water pumps, thermostats, hoses, and coolant levels while addressing issues such as engine overheating, coolant leaks, low coolant levels, and inefficient temperature regulation.

Wheel, Tire & Alignment Services

We inspect, diagnose, and maintain wheels, tires, and alignment systems including rims, tires, wheel bearings, suspension links, and alignment settings while addressing issues such as uneven tire wear, vibrations, pulling to one side, steering misalignment, and poor handling.

Car detailing

We provide professional car detailing services, including thorough cleaning, polishing, and protecting of both interior and exterior surfaces, such as paint, windows, upholstery, carpets, and trim while addressing dirt buildup, stains, scratches, and maintaining overall vehicle appearance and hygiene.

Preventive Maintenance

We perform comprehensive preventive maintenance by inspecting, diagnosing, and servicing key vehicle systems including engine, transmission, brakes, suspension, fluids, belts, filters, and electrical components to prevent breakdowns, improve performance, and extend vehicle lifespan.
